PhD student in theoretical and computational statistical physics  

LPTMS, Université Paris-Saclay, CNRS  
PMMH, ESPCI Paris, Université PSL, Sorbonne Université, Université Paris Cité, CNRS 

I study driven disordered systems, with a focus on finite-temperature creep, depinning, and amorphous plasticity.

My work combines numerical simulations, kinetic Monte Carlo methods, scaling analysis, and theoretical ideas from out-of-equilibrium statistical physics.
